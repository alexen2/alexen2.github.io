The proven process

We help companies create first-class products that meet our customers’ needs and further their business goals. We take full ownership of the product, helping you through the design stage and supporting you right up to the product launch date and beyond.

Discovery & Strategy. Understand your business, customers, product, and market. Initial research: business requirements review, product audit, market research (competitor research, etc.). Product Strategy: defining users (empathy map, personas), needs in context (storyboards, customer journey maps), existing or competitor solutions (heuristic analysis, feature matrix), framing the problem (POV statements).

Product definition and prototyping. Define the product: user flow based on the user scenarios, informational Architecture. Wireframing and interactive prototyping.

Visual design and finalizing user scenarios. Brand visual identity, high fidelity mockups, UI interactive prototype, UI kit and patterns libraries, specs and metrics. Your dev team will love our deliverables.

Delivering development assets, graphics, icons, etc.

Development support. It’s always important to manage the implementation process by designers, since it will help you to keep the design quality on mockups and transfer it to the real product. Only designer can see the user experience issues that can be critical for the final customer but might not be seen by the developer. Each of our packages includes 1 month’s support from our design team. If something changes down the road and the app needs slight adjustments—we’re here to help.
